OUR SERVICES

Micgra Tek is a global Structural Steel Detailing organization made up of a team of qualified and experienced engineers and detailers. Established in 2018, Micgra Tek has undertaken commercial and industrial projects for clients in all corners of the world.

Micgra Tek has the capability to produce complicated 2D and 3D models for contract drawings which include structural and architectural drawings. We generate Fabrication and Erection drawings using Tekla Structures and CAD. These drawings are accepted by Steel Fabricators and Erectors in North America.

Our repeat customer base is testament to our integrity, reliability, accuracy, and promptness. We follow AISC standards for all our work with multiple teams spread across geographies. We deploy the latest versions of software and hardware to provide our clients with 24/7 service around the globe.
